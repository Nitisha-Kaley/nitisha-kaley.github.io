BookIT

An AR Book Library App

Main Task

In this application, we are giving the users the experience of surfing and buying books from a bookstore without Interacting with them directly due to the pamdemic.

Objective

Scitentist have suggested that the world will go touchless in the coming years. So this app will help novel readers browse through bookstores without touching any of the book by using AR.

Typography & Colours

Bookmark Feature

Like a book but can't get to a store right now?
We got you covered!
Just bookmark your title and
we save it for the next time you
pay a visit to the local bookseller.

Scan book Feature

See a book that catches your fancy?
Simply pointing the camera at the book will give you all the information you need about the book.
Our advanced image recognition technology recognises your book choice and enables you to add the book you like in your shopping cart just with a thumbs up gesture. Going touch free just got more convenient!
